EXTREME VIDEO GAME DEVELOPMENT

DinoBoss is a video game development studio specialising in visceral, atmospheric and emergent experiences, which sometimes involve throwing goats off mountains.

Instead of using processes, meetings, planning and expert specialists, at DinoBoss games are made by the sheer determination of one man smashing his fists at a keyboard until polygons come raining forth. Low poly ones of course because aint nobody got time for texture mapping in this joint.

DinoBoss takes the U out of FUN because it didn’t need it anyway.


CURRENT PROJECTS

The current project being lovingly created at DinoBoss is goat grappling action game Hang Line.


Hang Line is an extreme-physics climbing game where you swing up dangerous mountain peaks and avoid ending up in hospital from falling rocks, avalanches and angry goats.

Check it out at hangline.com.

Coming soon for iPhone®, iPad® and Android®.


WHO IS DINOBOSS?

ED KAY - FOUNDER


A 15 year game design veteran, Ed Kay has been dreaming of making his own game since discovering the Doom level editor in 1996. But after a career of headshots, explosions and special moves spanning Cthulhu to Bulletstorm, it wasn't until having his dreams shattered from the Lucasarts apocalypse that he decided it was time to escape the constant loop of shipping and layoffs that plagues AAA console development. And thus DinoBoss was born.
